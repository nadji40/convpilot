FAQs

You've Got Questions. We've Got Answers.

How does the AI help with convertible bonds analysis?

Our AI analyzes convertible bonds market data, performance attribution, sector trends and portfolio risk metrics to provide daily insights, predictive analytics and optimization recommendations for professional portfolio managers.

What data sources do you use for convertible bonds?

We integrate with leading financial data providers including Bloomberg, Refinitiv and specialized convertible bonds databases to ensure accurate, real-time pricing, corporate actions and market data for comprehensive portfolio analysis.

Can I customize the analytics and reporting?

Yes, our platform offers extensive customization options including custom sector groupings, maturity buckets, risk metrics, performance attribution models and automated reporting schedules tailored to your specific investment strategy and compliance requirements.

How secure is my portfolio data?

We employ bank-grade security with end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication and SOC 2 compliance. Your portfolio data is stored in secure, geographically distributed data centers with 24/7 monitoring and regular security audits to ensure maximum protection.
