FAQs

You’ve Got Questions. We’ve Got AI-Powered Answers.

How does the AI actually help me trade?

Our AI analyzes your behavior, trade history and emotional patterns to provide feedback and prevent risky trades in real time.

Is this real money or simulated?

This is simulated trading. You’re trading in a risk-free environment with virtual funds, which allows you to practice strategies and develop consistency without putting real capital at risk.

Can I use my own broker?

No, you cannot connect your own broker. All trading is done through our provided simulated platform, ensuring consistent rules, data and evaluation conditions for every trader.

What happens if I hit the daily loss limit?

If you reach the daily loss limit, trading for that day will automatically stop to protect your account from further losses. You’ll be able to resume trading the next day once the limit resets.
